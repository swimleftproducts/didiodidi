didiodidi

did I or didn't I do it.

A simple, local-first tracker for recurring to-dos — built for physical-therapy exercises, but works for any daily routine. No account, no cloud database: everything lives on your phone.

How it works

Add your routine

Create tasks with a title, description, and which days of the week they're due — by hand, or by snapping a photo of an exercise sheet.

Check them off

Each day, see what's due and tap to mark it done. Three gentle reminders a day keep you on track.

Share your progress

One tap builds a snapshot of your last 7 days and gives you a link — like share.didiodidi.com/you-abc123 — so a therapist or friend can see how you're doing.
