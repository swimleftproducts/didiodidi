didiodidi

* did I do it or didn't I do it.

A simple, local-first tracker for any recurring to-do — exercises, medication, habits, chores, whatever you need to stay on top of. No account, no cloud database: everything lives on your phone.

How it works

Add your routine

Create tasks with a title, description, and which days of the week they're due — by hand, or by snapping a photo of a plan or checklist.

Check them off

Each day, see what's due and tap to mark it done. Three gentle reminders a day keep you on track.

Share your progress

One tap builds a snapshot of your last 7 days and gives you a link — like share.didiodidi.com/you-abc123 — so a coach, therapist, or friend can see how you're doing.
